A Ukranian-owned small business in the Greater Toronto Area that specializes in custom designed items for every occasion. Mriya Creations is based in the Tomken & East Gate area and has shipping and pickup options.

WE STAND WITH UKRAINE.

We will stand with Ukraine as it defends itself against Russian aggression, for as long as it takes. We stand united in our enduring support for Ukraine, rooted in our shared democratic values and interests, above all, respect for the UN Charter and the principles of territorial integrity and sovereignty. Below are some ways to show your support or learn more about the war against Ukraine.

Defend Ukraine

Defend Ukraine provides a variety of places and ways to support Ukraine and the ongoing relief efforts. Through this link below you can find ways to donate to the Military support, medical supplies, mental health services, and many more.

www.defendukraine.org/donate

Red Cross

The Canadian Red Cross is raising money with the Red Crescent Movement to respond to humanitarian needs and response efforts due to the current war on Ukraine. The money raised goes toward immediate and ongoing relief efforts, preparedness, and any other critical humanitarian activities needed.

www.donate.redcross.ca

Army SOS

Army SOS helps in the relief efforts in two ways. They have developed and distributed "Kropyva", an intelligence mapping software that is used by hundreds of units throughout Ukraine that does planning, calculations, and orientation. They also provide Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAV) that are designed and constructed by Army SOS providing aerial reconnaissance to help save lives.

www.armysos.com.ua/donate/
